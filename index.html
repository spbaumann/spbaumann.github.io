🌿 About

I'm a data and analytics professional focused on improving how people experience, understand, and use data to make decisions.

My background spans analytics, research, and data product design in complex organizational environments. I specialize in bridging the gap between raw data and real‑world decision‑making, which means I design metrics, dashboards, and supporting materials that make insights accessible, trustworthy, and actionable.

In my work, I focus heavily on data experience: modernizing analytics products, simplifying complex reporting, and partnering with stakeholders to ensure data tools actually support how decisions are made. I bring a strong research mindset to analytics work, using qualitative and quantitative inputs to inform what gets built, how it's presented, and how it evolves over time.

🌿 How I Work

I approach analytics as a product and a service—not just a technical output.

  • Decision‑first mindset I start by understanding the decisions people are trying to make, then work backward to define metrics, data models, and visuals that support those decisions.
  • User‑centered data design I regularly gather feedback from data users through interviews, surveys, and working sessions to improve usability, clarity, and trust in analytics products.
  • Translation between worlds I act as a bridge between technical teams and non‑technical stakeholders—translating business questions into data requirements, and analysis into clear, human‑readable insights.
  • Iterative and pragmatic I favor lightweight, iterative approaches: releasing useful versions early, documenting assumptions, and improving based on real usage rather than hypothetical needs.
